About Me

Since my early teenage years in the late 90's I've been enamored with the 'Net' as we called it back then. I never really gave thought to why we called it the Net or the Web until recently, but that name holds true more than anything I've ever heard. The Internet changed my life as well as everyone else's life as we use it for everything. I remember sites back in the day tracking private islands for sale and saying one day that will be me.

The ability to create value and wealth from seemingly nothing had me shocked. Somehow I always knew that I wanted to be involved with the creation of our online world. This is what lead me to Web Development, and my unending quest to learn as much as I can to be creative and add value for others.
